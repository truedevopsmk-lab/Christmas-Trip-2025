# ☕🥐 **Budapest Coffee + Pastry Crawl (Hotel Rum → City Highlights)**

**Total time:** 2.5–3 hours
**Walking distance:** ~2 km
**Stops:** 4 high-quality places (1 pastry-heavy, 3 coffee-focused)
**Cost:** 💸–💵 (very reasonable)

---

# ⭐ **🗺️ Route Overview**

1️⃣ Freyja – Croissant Story (Pastry)
2️⃣ My Little Melbourne (Coffee)
3️⃣ Fekete (Coffee + light pastry)
4️⃣ Artizán Bakery (Pastry finale)

Ends near: **St. Stephen’s Basilica & Christmas Market**
Perfect continuation after the crawl!

---

# 🥐 **Stop 1 — Freyja: Croissant Story**

📍 *Budapest, Akácfa u. 18*
🕒 **Time: 9:00–9:30 AM**
💵 Price: Mid-range

### What to try:

* Pistachio croissant
* Almond croissant
* Vanilla bean cream croissant
* Cardamom bun

🎯 **Why start here?**
Freyja has Budapest’s *best* croissants — buttery, flaky, perfect.
Start with pastries before caffeine overload.

---

# ☕ **Stop 2 — My Little Melbourne Coffee Brew Bar**

📍 *Madách Imre út 3*
🕒 **Time: 9:40–10:05 AM**
💸 Price: Budget

### Best orders:

* Flat white
* Espresso flight
* V60 pour-over

🎯 **Why here?**
The OG of Budapest specialty coffee.
Minimalistic, clean flavour profiles.

---

# ☕ **Stop 3 — Fekete Coffee**

📍 *Budapest, Múzeum krt. 5*
🕒 **Time: 10:15–10:45 AM**
💸 Price: Budget

### What to try:

* Cortado
* Batch brew (usually excellent)
* Light pastries (if you want something small)

🎯 **Why it’s great:**

* Beautiful courtyard
* Sits right in the heart of the city
* Excellent espresso

This is your comfortable “middle anchor” coffee stop.

---

# 🍰 **Stop 4 — Artizán Bakery (Pastry Finale!)**

📍 *Hold utca 3*
🕒 **Time: 11:00–11:30 AM**
💵 Price: Mid-range

### Must-try pastries:

* Pistachio pain suisse
* Chocolate swirl
* Seasonal brioche
* Apple & vanilla pastry

🎯 **Why finish here?**
Artizán is consistently ranked as one of the **top 2 bakeries in Budapest**, and it’s close to St. Stephen’s Basilica — perfect to continue exploring.

---

# ✨ Optional Bonus Stop

If you're still in the mood for something sweet or want one more coffee:

### **Espresso Embassy ☕ 💸 (near Parliament)**

One of the best specialty cafes in the city — clean, bright roasts.

### OR

### **Gelarto Rosa 🍨 💸**

For a creative **rose-shaped gelato** (veg-friendly).
