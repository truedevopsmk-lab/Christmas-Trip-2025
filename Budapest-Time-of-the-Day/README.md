# 🇭🇺 **Budapest — Detailed Time-of-Day Itinerary (Winter / December Edition)**

> **Sunrise in December:** ~7:25–7:45 AM
> **Sunset in December:** ~3:55–4:10 PM
> Expect it to be **cold, cloudy, sometimes foggy**, but sunsets over the Danube still look magical on clear days.

---

## **📅 Ideal Day Plan — Budapest**

| Time of Day                                  | Activities                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  | Why This Works in December                                                                                               |
| -------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |

## ⭐ **Top Sunrise Spots (December-Friendly)**

Even if cloudy, these are beautiful vantage points:

1. **Fisherman’s Bastion** → best for photos, arches frame the Parliament
2. **Gellért Hill – Citadel** → panoramic but colder + more windy
3. **Buda Castle Terrace** → easy access, less crowded
4. **Margaret Bridge** → Parliament views with reflections

---

## ⭐ **Top Sunset Spots**

1. **Parliament Riverbank (Pest side)**
2. **Castle Hill – near Matthias Church**
3. **Liberty Bridge**
4. **Danube River Cruise (heated cabin)**
